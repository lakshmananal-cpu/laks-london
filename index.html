Speaking Topics

What I speak about.

Four keynote topics for executive audiences navigating the AI transition — strategy grounded in production experience, not theory.

01

The Agentic Enterprise

AI has moved beyond chatbots and point solutions. Agentic systems — AI that can plan, reason, and orchestrate multi-step workflows — are reshaping how enterprises operate. This session explores what that shift means practically: new capabilities unlocked, new governance challenges created, and what separates organisations that will lead from those that will follow.

Keynote Strategy 50–75 min
02

AI Governance & Trust

As AI autonomy increases — from human-in-the-loop to human-on-the-loop — governance can't be an afterthought. Drawing on hands-on experience building production AI systems, this session makes the case for establishing accountability and oversight before regulation forces the issue, and what that actually looks like inside a real organisation.

Keynote Governance 45–60 min
03

From Models to Systems

Having access to an AI model and running an AI system at enterprise scale are very different things. This talk covers what the transition actually requires — the data foundations, integration architecture, evaluation frameworks, and operational disciplines that turn experiments into production-grade capability that delivers measurable business value.

Technical Operations 45–60 min
04

The Future of Work & AI

What does AI mean for talent, skills, and the workforce? Through the lens of someone building AI systems and an education marketplace, this session addresses the honest version of that question — not the utopian or dystopian extremes, but the practical reality of what changes, what stays, and what leaders need to do to bring people with them.

Leadership Talent 45–60 min
